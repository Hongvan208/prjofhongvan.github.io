Mr. Eric devoted decades of his life to creating opportunities for students from rural areas, ethnic communities, and low-income families. His vision was simple yet powerful: "Education can change a life, and one changed life can change a community."

To many students, he was more than a sponsor; he was a mentor, a father figure, a guiding light, and a source of endless encouragement. He nurtured the dreams of those who had little hope, showing that dedication and compassion could transform lives.

His kindness reached thousands of children across Vietnam, including me. His belief in the potential of every student continues to inspire us to work harder, dream bigger, and live with compassion.

Even in the toughest moments, Mr. Eric's vision remained unwavering. Through his programs and personal guidance, countless students were able to overcome challenges, gain confidence, and pursue higher education. His philosophy was clear: provide opportunity, instill values, and empower young people to lead positive change.

I will always remember Mr. Eric with profound gratitude. His dedication, humility, and endless support have left a lasting impact not only on my life but on the lives of thousands of students who continue to follow the path he illuminated. His legacy of hope, education, and love will endure for generations.
